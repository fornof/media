The Story

It started in 1985 with a telephone and a Smith-Corona typewriter.

Now, impact creative media, inc. employs a team of top media professionals from New York to Los Angeles. (Okay, technically, it’s Buffalo, but it’s still New York.)

Whether you need something as simple as a 30 second radio commercial, or as complex as a theatrical film project, the team at impact creative media is here for you.

Our work is on time, on budget, and on target.

Simply put, we love what we do. Just give us a call. And put our passion to work for you.

(And don’t worry. We’ve upgraded the typewriter. )

Strong Families Promo

Here’s a promo produced by the team for best-selling author Dr. John Trent. Since the course is all about coaching families, we had John and his fellow presenters dress up as coaches. The audience members got into the act, garbed in football jerseys. And we converted a beautiful chapel into a full-blown locker room set. Take a look. Multi-camera shoot by Larry Douglas/Access Media Group. Custom open by Stephen Vidano Films. Music custom composed by John Campbell.
